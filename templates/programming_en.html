Programming

I am a self-taught developer working mostly with JavaScript and Python. I got into coding during my time at university. While studying for becoming a teacher in mathematics I also became very interested in Linux and open source software. I pursued this passion in my master thesis, developing Kubos, an open-source CAD application written in Python. Thanks to its thought-through software design it consists of just 4000 lines of code.

More recently I turned my attention to interactive web content and web applications. As a personal project, I created a 3D version of the game 2048 and I am working on two web-based 3D modeling applications: Kubos Blocks and Kubos Solids. Furthermore, I've done projects involving Django, AngularJS and Bootstrap.
